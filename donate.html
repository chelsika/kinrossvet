Kinross Veterinary Center Hero Fund

 

Looking back in time, no matter how far you go, there are tragedies that can overwhelm us. Right now we are surrounded by hurricane victims, shooting sprees and raging fires. So many are in need of help.

 

In times of great need many reach out to share love through donations, giving of themselves and spreading the word to bring others forward to join in. Each corner of the world has families with limited funds living day by day. Simple gestures can make an incredible difference when a beloved pet becomes ill or injured. Pets are not a privilege, but complete us and enrich our lives. To correspond with our opening we are creating a Hero Fund to assist those in financial need, when a little extra financially can make the difference. If you are able, please donate, make a difference and Be Their Hero.
