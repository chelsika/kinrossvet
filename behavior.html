Helping pets live happy, healthy, full lives

 

We all love our pets and want what’s best for them throughout their lives. We aren’t always equipped with the right tools, information, education, or resources to truly fulfill that goal. Assistance with experts well trained in behavior is the most appropriate step to continue the human-animal bond in a positive fashion. This is where Fear Free and Fear Free Happy Homes come in.

 

Become a Fear Free Happy Homes member today, and experience all that Fear Free Happy Homes has to offer: https://fearfreehappyhomes.com/

 

See also below several websites, each with a multitude of videos created by behavioral professionals to better serve you, providing creative training and suggestions in many areas where you may be struggling with your pet.

 

Fear Free

 

The Fear Free mission is to prevent and alleviate fear, anxiety and stress in pets by inspiring and educating the people that care for them. The focus is on the human-animal bond, with their goal to help you develop a powerful, life-long relationship with your dog or cat.

 

Fear Free is an education company dedicated to providing resources, tools and knowledge to both pet professionals and pet owners to look after our pets’ emotional and physical well-being, and to provide pets with long, happy, healthy lives. Fear Free is dedicated to creating a world where every pet experiences Fear Free at every stage in his life – from shelters and breeders, to homes, veterinary visits, trainers, groomers, pet sitter, dog parks, transportation, enrichment and more. Kinross Veterinary Center staff have participated in the Fear Free training program. www.fearfreepets.com
